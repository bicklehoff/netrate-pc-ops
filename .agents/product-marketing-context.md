## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Independent mortgage broker with a live rate tool — see real wholesale pricing before you apply.
**What it does:** NetRate Mortgage shops across 11 wholesale lenders to find borrowers the best rate. The website features a live rate comparison tool that shows actual wholesale pricing — rate, points, lender credits, and monthly payment — before the borrower shares any personal information.
**Product category:** Mortgage broker (how borrowers search: "mortgage rates," "refinance rates," "Colorado mortgage broker")
**Product type:** Financial service + digital rate tool
**Business model:** Commission on funded loans (broker compensation from wholesale lender). No fees to borrower for rate shopping or quotes. Revenue comes from loan closings. Lean cost structure (2 people, no branch overhead) = better pricing passed to borrower.

## Target Audience
**Target borrower type:** Homeowners (refinance) and buyers (purchase) in CO, TX, OR. Primarily Colorado (92% of 2025 volume). Loan amounts $75K–$1.2M.
**Decision-makers:** The borrower directly — no realtor intermediary. Often one half of a couple doing the research.
**Primary use case:** Rate shopping — "Am I getting a good deal?" or "Can I save money by refinancing?"

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Mortgage borrowers can't get real pricing without first giving up personal information and enduring a sales process.
**Why current solutions fall short:**
- Banks show you one option (theirs) and won't negotiate
- Big online lenders (Rocket, LoanDepot) use bait-and-switch rates — the advertised rate isn't what you'll get
- Most brokers require an application before showing real numbers
- Rate comparison sites (Bankrate, NerdWallet) show estimated averages, not actual available rates
- Realtor-referred lenders are chosen for the referral relationship, not the borrower's best interest
**What it costs them:**
- Overpaying by 0.25–0.50% on rate = thousands over the life of the loan
- Wasted time on applications that go nowhere
- Surprise fees at closing
- Decision paralysis from lack of transparent information
**Emotional tension:**
- Distrust: "Are they giving me the real rate or just hooking me?"
- Anxiety: "Am I getting ripped off and I don't even know it?"
- Frustration: "Why do I have to give my SSN just to see a rate?"
- Overwhelm: "There are too many options and I can't compare them"

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct competitors:** Other local mortgage brokers in CO/TX/OR — most lack online rate tools, rely on phone calls and realtor referrals. Falls short because: no upfront rate transparency, old-school relationship model.
**Secondary competitors:** Big online lenders (Rocket Mortgage, Better.com, LoanDepot) — different approach, same problem. Falls short because: bait-and-switch pricing, call center experience, not actually personalized, push you into their single product.
**Indirect competitors:** Going direct to a bank (Wells Fargo, Chase) — one lender's rates only. Falls short because: no shopping, no negotiation, limited programs, retail markup on rates.
**Also competing with:** Inertia — "I'll refinance later" or "My rate is probably fine."

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Live rate tool with real wholesale pricing (not estimates or averages)
- See rates BEFORE applying or sharing personal info
- 11 wholesale lenders shopped simultaneously
- Direct-to-consumer (no realtor referral fees baked in)
- Lean operation = lower costs = better pricing
- Break-even analysis and recoup calculations included automatically
**How we do it differently:** We give away the information other lenders use as leverage. Most lenders make you apply to see your rate — we show it to you first, with full math, so you can decide if it's worth your time.
**Why that's better:** Borrowers make better decisions with better information. And when they do choose to work with us, there are no surprises — they already know the numbers.
**Why customers choose us:** "He was correct and there were no surprises." "The best rates around. Very knowledgeable, totally straight forward." "He worked hard to get me the best rate."

**Glossary:**
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Wholesale rate | The rate lenders offer to brokers, typically lower than retail (direct-to-consumer bank) rates |
| Par rate | The rate where there's no cost and no credit — your baseline for comparison |
| Lender credit | Money the lender gives you (to offset closing costs) when you choose a rate above par |
| Discount points | Money you pay upfront to buy a lower rate (below par) |
| LLPA | Loan-Level Price Adjustment — how credit score, LTV, property type affect your rate |
| Recoup period | How many months until refinance savings cover the closing costs |
| DSCR | Debt Service Coverage Ratio — investor loan qualification based on rental income vs. expenses |
