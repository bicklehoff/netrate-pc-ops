## Key Rules

1. **Aggregators first.** Data Axle and Neustar feed 100+ directories each. These are the highest-leverage updates.
2. **Name only on GBP.** Never change name + address + phone at the same time. One change at a time.
3. **No edits 2 weeks before rename.** Google flags accounts with lots of recent activity.
4. **Request live video call.** If video verification is triggered, request the live call option. It has a higher approval rate than recorded video.
5. **Don't spam resubmissions.** If rejected, wait at least 2 weeks before trying again.
6. **Signage matters.** Have a permanent "NetRate Mortgage" plaque visible at the office address on file.
